About My Paper Shop.Com

Since 2001 My Paper Shop has offered an extensive selection of bulk paper products and discount party supplies that are perfect for a multitude of special occasions and themed events. We are paper party suppliers and our products are sold in bulk quantities at bargain prices to both individuals and businesses worldwide. Whether you are planning a special theme baby shower or birthday celebration we can help you find party supplies and decorations to fit every taste, style and budget. As discount party suppliers, we offer theme party ensembles that are complete with paper napkins, plates, cups, plastic tablecloths, invitations, decorations and more.
