Description

Gloriously showcasing the art of legendary Star Wars artist Steve Anderson - commissioned exclusively for this collection - this first-ever HO-scale Star Wars train collection celebrates these epic films like never before! Your dramatic collection begins with Issue One, a diesel locomotive. Soon, your collection continues with Issue Two, the coordinating Star Wars engine, which includes a FREE 14-piece HO gauge track, Issue Three, a dome car, which includes a FREE power pack and speed controller, and Issue Four, “The Force Awakens”. Additional train cars, each a separate issue, will follow.‡

Officially licensed by Lucasfilm Ltd. and available only from The Bradford Exchange, Hawthorne Village Division, each car in this real working electric train collection features scenes of dramatic movie moments and favorite Star Wars characters like Darth Vader, Storm Troopers and more. Plus, many of these powerful scenes glow in the dark! Strong demand is expected for this unique Star Wars collectible, perfect for any Star Wars fan. Don't wait, order now!

Features
This exclusive Star Wars train collection from The Bradford Exchange features:
  • First-ever HO-scale Star Wars train collection featuring the specially commissioned art of legendary Star Wars artist Steve Anderson, available only from The Bradford Exchange, Hawthorne Village Division
  • Officially licensed by Lucasfilm Ltd.
  • Included free 14-piece HO gauge track with Issue Two and a power pack and speed controller with Issue Three
  • HO-scale train collection is carefully handcrafted with precision detailing and authentic features including a solid metal chassis and steel alloy wheels to bring you years of enjoyment
  • Each train car in this collection features dramatic movie moments and favorite characters like Darth Vader and more all rendered by artist Steve Anderson specially for this train
  • Many powerful scenes glow in the dark for further enjoyment
  • Lights up! The locomotive headlight and passenger train cars really light up for a fully illuminated display of the excitement of the Star Wars films
  • The heirloom-quality trains are precisely scaled; train is designed to run on HO gauge track
  • Certificates of Authenticity
  • Individual train cars run on HO train track, complete oval track measures 38" x 47"; 96.5 cm x 119.4 cm
