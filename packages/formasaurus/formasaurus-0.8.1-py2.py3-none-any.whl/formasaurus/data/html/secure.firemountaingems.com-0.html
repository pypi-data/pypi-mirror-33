Your Shopping Cart

Fire Mountain Gems and Beads hopes to be your favorite supplier for your creative jewelry-making needs. We have over 116,000 jewelry-making supplies for you to explore.

We find that the most accurate way to maintain a shopping cart for you is by the use of a unique identifier that is sent to your computer. This unique identifier is also known as a Cookie. Think of this as a name badge that is handed to you when you enter.

With the unique identifier enabled you will be able to add plenty of interesting jewelry-making supplies to your shopping cart. Even if you are going to leave the site for a while and come back later, your shopping cart will be just like you left it. The shopping cart will be available up to 120 days after your last visit.

To create a shopping cart on this site, your browser must have cookies enabled. If you would like to do this now, we have some very easy instructions here.

If you still have questions about your shopping cart, please do not hesitate to contact us.
