B-52s With The Wild Crowd-Live In Athens, GA DVD
Qty:


With The Wild Crowd-Live In Athens, GA is the first official B-52s live DVD, a sizzling set recorded in the band's hometown on February 11, 2011. The show marked the 34th anniversary of their first-ever concert on Valentine's Day 1977. Wig-wearing, boa-draped, glitter-covered fans came from near and far to celebrate this historic event, turning Athens' Classic Center into a cosmic dancehall. This DVD includes the classics "Love Shack," "Rock Lobster," "Give Me Back My Man," "Funplex," "Roam," "Planet Claire," "Party Out Of Bounds," "Mesopotamia" and many many more.
