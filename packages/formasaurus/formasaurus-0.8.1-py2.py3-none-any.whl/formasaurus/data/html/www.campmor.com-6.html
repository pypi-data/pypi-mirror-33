If you're not happy with your purchase from Campmor, then we're not happy either.


Your satisfaction is our goal. You may return any item*, in new, sellable condition, for any reason, within one year of purchase, when accompanied by your receipt or packing slip.

New condition means:
    - tags still on
    - packaging intact
    - unworn
    - unwashed
    - unstained
    - unscuffed
    - no tears
    - no odors
If you've used an item and find it to be defective, we will exchange or repair the item within one year of purchase date, when returned to us along with your receipt.

About Teva Hurricane 2 Sports Sandals - Kids

 

Function without frills for Teva kids. Light and durable, the Teva Kid's Hurricane 2 is built on a high-traction Durabrasion Rubber™ outsole and features classic Teva sandal strapping architecture. The Teva Innovative Design Elements (T.I D.E.) Lab is where design and technology combine to create footwear sure to make your outdoor adventures better.
  • T.I.D.E. Comfort construction based on 30 years of Teva experience.
  • T.I.D.E. Hydro open-toe construction so water doesn't pool underfoot.
  • Quick drying webbing upper.
  • Universal strapping system with three points of adjustment for a custom fit.
  • Padded heel strap.
  • Microban® zinc based anti-microbial protection.
  • Compression-molded EVA midsole.
  • Soft Shoc Pad™ in the heel for shock absorption.
  • T.I.D.E. Grip - Durabrasion Rubber™ outsole gives you a solid balance of performance and durability without being overkill for everyday activities.
  • Avg. wt. 6.2 oz.
